# Vocabulary
The word apple is a fruit.
The word banana is a yellow fruit.
The word cat is a small animal.
The word dog is a loyal animal.
The word sun is a star.
The word moon orbits the earth.
The word computer is a machine.
The word brain is an organ.
The word code is instructions.
The word happy means feeling good.
The word sad means feeling bad.

# Grammar rules
A noun is a person place or thing.
A verb is an action word.
An adjective describes a noun.
An adverb describes a verb.
A sentence must have a subject and a verb.
The past tense of go is went.
The past tense of see is saw.
The past tense of eat is ate.
The plural of cat is cats.
The plural of child is children.

# Common Phrases
Hello means greetings.
Goodbye means farewell.
Thank you means expressing gratitude.
How are you asks about well being.
I am fine means I am well.
What is your name asks for identity.
Nice to meet you is a polite greeting.

# Complex Concepts
Democracy is government by the people.
Science is the study of the natural world.
History is the study of the past.
Mathematics is the science of numbers.
Language is a method of communication.
Artificial Intelligence is machine intelligence.
