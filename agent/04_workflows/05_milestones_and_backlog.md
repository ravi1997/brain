# Workflow: Milestones and Backlog

## Purpose

Turn the design into an actionable, prioritized task list.

## Inputs

- Approved Architecture and SRS.

## Procedure

1. **Adopt Profile**: `profile_planner.md`.
2. **Milestone Creation**: Define 3-5 major phases (e.g., "M1: Core Engine", "M2: CLI", "M3: API").
3. **Task Decomposition**: Break Phase 1 into granular tasks (not exceeding 4 hours each).
4. **State Seeding**: Populate `agent/09_state/BACKLOG_STATE.md`.
5. **Prerequisites**: Identify task dependencies.

## STOP Condition

- Backlog is populated with at least one complete Milestone.
